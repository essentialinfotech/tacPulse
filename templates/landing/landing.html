Working Services

TAC-Pulse ERS is a state-of-art Private Emergency Response Service, operating in the greater Wolmaransstad (Dr. Kenneth Kaunda District) areas within a radius of 250 km of its surroundings. We strive to provide a world-class, life-saving service to the communities we serve, with only highly trained and skilled paramedics operating out of our ambulances which are equipped with the latest and most advanced emergency medical equipment and technology available. We pride ourselves on our excellent response times and highest quality of medical care, and at all times treat the patients entrusted to us with the utmost professionalism and above all with dignity and compassion. At TAC-Pulse ERS we believe that we are tactically linking the communities we serve.

About Us

With a solid foundation of operational experience that spans well over 10 years working for the US Departments of State and Defence, NATO as well as other international agencies in war-torn, rural Afghanistan and Iraq, TAC-Pulse Emergency Response Services was born into a community, desperately in need of emergency medical service within an extremely challenging environment, both logically and economically. In 2006, using their vast wartime experience and skills gained in these fields; they began their survey study on the Wolmaransstad and surrounding areas. This study became more comprehensive during the years to follow with an in-depth look at: Compensation scales and service feasibility Available Government resources (EMS & Health Services) Available Private Sector resources (EMS) Community interviews/survey and Known/Available accident and injury numbers review The need for a state-of-the-art private Emergency Medical Service was evident within all facets of the study. TAC-Pulse ERS officially launched on January 1, 2015, becoming Wolmaransstad’s first, professional private ambulance service. We have since expanded our services to Johannesburg areas and continue to grow our footprint.
